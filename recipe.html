Nigerian Jollof Recipe

A Vibrant Nigerian dish made with rice, tomato and spices

Recipe Information

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Cooking Time: 40 minutes

Servings: 5

Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of rice
  • 5 large blended tomatoes
  • 4 red bell peppers (blended with the tomatoes)
  • 2 medium chopped onions
  • 2 tbsp tpmato paste
  • 1 tbsp thyme
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 2 seasoning cubes
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • Chicken stock

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pot, add chopped onions and fry till soft.
  2. Add tomato paste, blended tomatoes and pepper (in that order). Fry for 10-15 minutes.
  3. Season with thyme, curry powder, bay leaves, seasoning cubes and salt.
  4. Pour in chicken stock or water and bring to a boil.
  5. Wash the rice and add it into the pot. Stir well.
  6. Cover with foil and lid, then cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes.
  7. Fluff the rice and serve it.

Tips

You can add grilled chicken or asude of fried plantain to make it extra yummy. Also stir the rice as it cooks occasionally so it doesn't burn too much.
